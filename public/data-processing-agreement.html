Data Processing Agreement

Effective Date: November 2025

1. Purpose

This Data Processing Agreement (DPA) specifies our data protection obligations regarding the processing of merchant data through the Smart Product Sync app.

2. Data Processing Scope

We process the following data on your behalf:

  • Product information (titles, descriptions, images)
  • Inventory data and stock levels
  • Product variants and options
  • Store identification information

3. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Data backup and recovery procedures

4. Data Subject Rights

We assist you in fulfilling data subject requests in accordance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection laws.

5. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

6. Subprocessors

We may use the following subprocessors to provide our services:

  • Cloud hosting providers for data storage
  • Monitoring and analytics services

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Upon app uninstallation, all your data is permanently deleted within 24 hours.
